Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy clearly explains what data Sudoku Fight collects, how we use it, and what your rights are as a user.

1. Data controller

The controller responsible for processing your personal data is:

  • Name: Javier Benítez (sole trader)
  • Contact: privacy@sudokufight.com
  • Country: Spain

2. Data we collect

We collect only what is necessary for the app to work:

  • Username — chosen by you when you sign up. This is the only identifier visible in the app and in games. We do not ask for your real name.
  • Email address — collected at registration. Used exclusively for important service communications (service notices, account recovery). It is never shown publicly or to other users. You can update it at any time from Settings → Change email address.
  • Password — stored hashed with bcrypt. We never see it in plain text.
  • Game data — results, times, moves and gameplay statistics.
  • Push notification token — to send you game alerts (only if you authorise it).
  • Device identifier — used by analytics and advertising services (see section 5).

3. Legal basis for processing

We process your data under the following legal bases (GDPR, art. 6):

  • Contract performance — account data (including email) and game data are necessary to provide the service.
  • Consent — push notifications and personalised advertising require your prior authorisation, which you can withdraw at any time.
  • Legitimate interest — aggregate and anonymous analysis of app usage to improve the service.

4. Who we share your data with

We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties. We do work with the following service providers:

  • Own server — account and game data are stored on a private server under our exclusive control.
  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising (in the free version, and in rewarded ads the user voluntarily chooses to watch). May collect IDFA/GAID to serve relevant ads, subject to your consent via our consent popup. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry — technical error reporting to improve app stability. Reports do not include game data or personal identifiers. Sentry Privacy Policy.
  • Expo / Expo Push Service — push notification delivery. Expo Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising and tracking

The free version of Sudoku Fight shows ads managed by Google AdMob. Before showing personalised ads we ask for your explicit consent via a popup (required by EU law — TCF 2.0 — and by Apple on iOS via the ATT framework).

If you decline consent, non-personalised ads will be shown. The Premium version removes all automatic ads (banners and interstitials). Rewarded ads are voluntary: in both the free and Premium versions, they are only shown if you choose to watch one in exchange for an in-game benefit (for example, an extra hint).

You can withdraw your consent at any time in your device settings.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Account and game data: for as long as your account is active.
  • Email address: for as long as your account is active. You can update it at any time from Settings.
  • After account deletion: all your data (including your email) is permanently and immediately deleted from our servers. This action is irreversible.
  • Error reports (Sentry): automatically deleted after 90 days.

7. Your rights

As a user in the European Union you have the right to:

  • Access — know what data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data, including your email address (Settings → Change email address).
  • Erasure — delete your account and all your data directly from the app (Settings → Delete account).
  • Portability — receive your data in a readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent — at any time for notifications and personalised advertising.

To exercise any right, write to us at privacy@sudokufight.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Spain: AEPD.

8. Security

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt.
  • Communications encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication tokens with automatic expiry.
  • Restricted access to server data.
  • Email address is never exposed in the app interface or public APIs.

9. Minors

Sudoku Fight is not directed at children under 13. If you become aware that a minor has provided us with data without parental consent, please contact us to delete it immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when necessary. We will notify you of important changes via the app. The date of last update is always shown at the top of this page.

11. Contact

For any privacy questions: privacy@sudokufight.com
